Representative Matters

  • Trial counsel representing California in multiple bench trials for California Department of Toxic Substances Control v. NL Industries, Inc., No. 2:20-cv-11293-SVW-JPRx (C.D. Cal.), a lawsuit pertaining to the largest contaminated site cleanup in state history. Successfully established strict, joint, and several liability against key defendants; defeated the sole remaining counterclaim; and defeated all key affirmative defenses.
  • Counsel for several states and local governments in groundbreaking litigation against major fossil fuel companies to redress injuries caused by climate-related deception and concealment.
  • Counsel for several states and local governments in major lawsuits against manufacturers of per- and poly-fluoroalkyl substances (PFAS) and PFAS-containing products.
  • Counsel for Contra Costa County, Marin County, and San Mateo County, California, as well as a number of municipalities in the counties, for claims arising out of polychlorinated biphenyl (PCB) contamination that impairs San Francisco Bay.
  • Advisor to a governor's office in a highly sensitive multi-forum dispute posing difficult questions of first impression.
