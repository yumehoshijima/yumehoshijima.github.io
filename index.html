Hello! I'm Yume (pronounced "you may"), an attorney based in the San Francisco Bay Area. I work for Sher Edling LLP, a boutique law firm that represents states, counties, cities, and other public entities across the United States in high-impact climate change and environmental litigation. I'm an all-rounder who leads teams across all stages of litigation from pre-suit investigations to appeals, with an especially heavy emphasis on critical motions.

I was born in Japan and grew up in San Diego, where I spent much of my time at the beach. I moved east to Yale for college, graduate school, and law school. Then, I clerked for a couple of years in Los Angeles and Salt Lake City before joining Sher Edling. In my spare time, I swim, cycle, hike, and am planning to re-learn how to surf after a long hiatus. I'm a news junkie who prizes and hoards interesting and occasionally useful information. Please use this website to learn about me, and please don't hesitate to get in touch.
